9 Ways to Beat Revision Stress

by @inner_drive | www.innerdrive.co.uk
  1. Do the actual work - revise
  2. When really stressed, talk to someone about it
  3. Get some freash air each day
  4. Stick to regular mealtimes
  5. Do something to switch off an hour before bed
  6. Don't dwell on worst case scenarios
  7. A good sleep the night before is better than last minute cramming
  8. Once you've done the exam, move on to the next one
  9. Don't aim for perfection - it's a myth and doesn't exist
